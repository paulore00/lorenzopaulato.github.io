Nisus

Not just a lamp

With H.E. Pianezza, M. Trinchero, J.J. Ricci
My Role Concept Dev., 3D Modeling, Engineering
Year 2024

Concept

Managing time is complex — balancing focus and rest even more. What if a lamp could shape our day simply by doing what it was made to do? Nisus is a desk lamp with an integrated timer: one gesture activates a 25-minute Pomodoro cycle, helping structure work sessions without a screen in sight.

Interaction

A slider adjusts brightness. An hourglass button activates the focus timer. Everything within reach — no app, no screen, no distraction.
