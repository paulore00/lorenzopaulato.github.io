Monolith

Speaker

Type Personal Project
My Role Concept Dev., 3D Modeling, Rendering
Year 2025

Concept

Development of a speaker designed to blend naturally into contemporary spaces, through essential geometries and clean lines. Monolith embraces the idea that audio equipment can be as considered as any piece of furniture — present without imposing.

Solid Presence.
Refined Sound.

Form

Three stacked volumes — each housing a driver. The tapering geometry grounds the object while the rounded edges soften its industrial character. A monolith that listens.
